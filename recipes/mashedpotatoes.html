No No, Mashed Potatoes

This are my favorite way to enjoy potatoes. And the plus side, no need to mash the potatoes.

Ingredients

  • Campbell's Condensed Cream of Celery Soup
  • Campbell's Chicken Gravy
  • Milk
  • Potatoes
  • Sliced Onion
  • Cooked Ham
  • Cheddar Cheese

Directions

  1. Step 1

    Stir soup, gravy and milk in bowl. Layer half the potatoes, onion, ham and soup mixture in 13x9x2" shallow baking dish. Repeat layers. Cover.

  2. Step 2

    Bake at 375 degrees F for 40 minutes. Uncover and bake 25 minutes. Top with cheese. Bake 5 minutes more or until potatoes are tender and cheese melts. Let stand 10 minutes. Enjoy!
